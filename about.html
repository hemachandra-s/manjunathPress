The Principles We Print By

We believe printing is an artistic pursuit, which is why we hold ourselves to strict standards.

1. Hand-Mixed Colors

We do not use standard ink cartridges. Every ink shade used for our screen-printing runs is hand-mixed using base pigments to ensure that the crimson, red, gold, or burgundy matches your color coordinates exactly.

2. Material Integrity

Our paper is selected for its texture, stiffness, and chemical purity. We primarily print on FSC-certified cotton boards and acid-free sheets, ensuring your cards will not warp, yellow, or fade over decades.

3. Structural Design

An invitation is an unfolding narrative. From the box structure and sleeve slides to pocket inserts and RSVP card envelopes, we carefully design the opening sequence to generate maximum excitement.
